The isolation is killing you inside and all you want is to feel a connection with somebody else . 
You now have little self-esteem left inside and have dark thoughts about life and relationships . 
However you still have that crave of friends inside of you , even if you won't admit that to yourself anymore . 
And even if you do have a friend , you still feel lonely because you have a hard time letting people in since you have been isolated so long .
